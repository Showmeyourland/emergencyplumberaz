## What I'm NOT recommending

These came up in research but I'm SKIPPING:

- **Property type pages** (sell mobile home, sell condo). The searches are buyer-intent ("mobile homes for sale"), not seller-intent. Wrong audience.
- **National-scale keywords** ("sell your home fast for cash" — 14,800 MSV). We're AZ-focused; competing nationally would dilute our topical signal.
- **State-level pages for non-AZ states** (the multi-state expansion was the OLD plan). With "AZ Home Cash Guy" branding, we stay AZ-focused. If you want multi-state, that's a separate site.
- **Property auction / probate auction pages**. Buyer intent, not us.
- **iBuyer / direct competitor brand-name pages** (Opendoor Phoenix, Offerpad Phoenix). Risk of trademark issues + audience is researching competitors, not converting on us.
